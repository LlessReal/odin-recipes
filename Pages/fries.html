Description

A crunchy delight that you'll enjoy alot

Ingredients

  • 2 cups warm water
  • â…“ cup white sugar
  • 2 large russet potatoes - peeled, and sliced into 1/4 inch strips
  • 6 cups vegetable oil for frying
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Stir together warm water and sugar in a medium bowl. Soak potatoes in water mixture for 15 minutes.
  3. Remove potatoes from water and dry thoroughly on paper towels. Heat oil in a deep-fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. Cook potatoes in hot oil until golden, 5 to 6 minutes. Drain on paper towels and season with salt.
